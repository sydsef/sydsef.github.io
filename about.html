Who We Are

The Pickled Peach is a fun place for lunch and dinner, serving simple, honest food that tastes great. We offer a changing menu of handcrafted sandwiches, soups and salads with meats roasted in-house and vegetables grown by local farmers. You'll find gluten-free and vegetarian options, alongside delicious homemade cookies and cakes, carefully selected artisan groceries, and take-away dinners.

We believe that sharing great food with our customers helps build lasting relationships and a strong community, and that service is about loving and caring for those around us. We look forward to serving you.

Our Story

After falling in love with the charming college town of Davidson, N.C., Travis and Jen Jentz moved from Michigan to start a life together. They soon married and had two boys. With a master's degree in hospitality and experience in the restaurant business between the two of them, they were ready to pursue their passion in the form of starting a restaurant. When a space opened up on South Main Street and they found that there were plenty of local farms in the surrounding area, they knew it was meant to be. The Pickled Peach was truly created by love and fate.

Farm to Table

All of our food is locally sourced and the majority is made from scratch. We use ingredients from local farms, all of which are listed on a chalkboard in store. Through ordering your food at the counter, you are able to witness the preparation of your food fresh and on the spot. This type of service also eliminates all issues with special dietary needs, for you as the customer requests it upfront.
